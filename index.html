My Services

Tier 1: Premium Translation Service

Description: This tier offers the highest quality translation, ensuring not only linguistic accuracy but also tone and cultural nuances are perfectly matched. The translation is proofread multiple times by both the primary translator and a secondary proofreader who is a native English speaker. This tier includes multiple rounds of edits and adjustments to ensure the highest level of accuracy and client satisfaction.

Best For: High-stakes documents where precision and tone are paramount, such as legal documents, marketing materials, and literary works.

Turnaround Time: Longest, due to the comprehensive process involved.

Price Range: Highest, reflecting the extensive work and expertise involved.

Tier 2: Professional Translation Service

Description: This tier provides professional-grade translation with attention to accuracy and readability. The translation is proofread by the translator for linguistic accuracy and by a native speaker for natural language flow, with one round of edits included.

Best For: Business documents, technical manuals, and general content where accuracy is important but the budget is more constrained.

Turnaround Time: Moderate, balancing thoroughness with efficiency.

Price Range: Mid-range, offering professional quality at a more accessible price point.

Tier 3: Standard Translation Service

Description: This tier offers a reliable translation that conveys the correct meaning of the original text, suitable for straightforward content. The translation undergoes a basic proofreading process by the translator for grammatical accuracy and clarity.

Best For: Internal communications, straightforward informational content, and any material where basic understanding is the priority over stylistic nuances.

Turnaround Time: Faster, as the process is more streamlined.

Price Range: Lower, designed for projects with limited budgets or where high-end precision is not critical.

Tier 4: Budget Translation Service

Description: This tier is designed for clients with tight budgets, providing a translation that is accurate but may not fully capture the tone or cultural nuances of the source material. Proofreading is limited to a single review by the translator.

Best For: Informal or non-critical communications, simple texts, and clients who prioritize cost over perfection.

Turnaround Time: Fastest, owing to a simplified process.

Price Range: Most affordable, reflecting the basic service level.

Additional Notes: Clients can request specific services like additional proofreading or rush delivery for an added fee, allowing for customization within each tier. All tiers come with a guarantee of confidentiality, ensuring clients' sensitive information is always protected. Regardless of the tier chosen, all clients receive dedicated support to address any concerns or questions throughout the translation process.
